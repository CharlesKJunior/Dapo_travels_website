About Dapo Travels

Your trusted partner for premium travel services in Uganda

Our Story

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Dapo Travels began as a small fleet of vehicles serving corporate clients in Kampala. Our founder, David Apollo, recognized the need for reliable, professional transportation services that combined luxury with affordability.

What started as a modest operation with just three vehicles has grown into one of Kampala's most trusted travel service providers. Today, we operate a modern fleet of over 50 vehicles and serve hundreds of satisfied clients monthly.

Our success stems from an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, safety, and continuous improvement. We've maintained our core values while adapting to the evolving needs of our clients in Uganda's dynamic transportation landscape.

To provide reliable, comfortable, and professional transportation services that enhance our clients' mobility and contribute to their success. We strive to set the standard for excellence in Uganda's travel industry through innovation, integrity, and exceptional service.
